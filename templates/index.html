About nit_connect

nit_connect has been at the forefront of networking solutions for over a decade. As integrators of communication networks, we focus on advanced IP-based networking solutions that drive business success.

We started with structured cabling activities but quickly evolved to higher OSI layers, responding to increasing demands for end-to-end solutions. Our expertise spans multiple market segments with references in hospitality, finance, education, and manufacturing sectors.

We leverage the power of your network infrastructure through the integration and convergence of voice, data, and video traffic on the backbone of your information system.
